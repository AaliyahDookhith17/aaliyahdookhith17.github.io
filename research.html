Research

Our research combines dynamic bonds and polymer mechanochemistry to understand the molecular origins of fracture and flow in dynamic polymers.

Mechanical Recycling of Thermoplastics

We investigate how molecular damage accumulates during polymer processing. By introducing dynamic sacrificial bonds and mechanochemical probes into thermoplastics, we seek to understand and mitigate bond scission during extrusion, enabling more processable plastics.

Tough and Reprocessable Vitrimers

We focus on understanding how network architecture and bond-exchange chemistry influence toughness, stress redistribution, and long-term performance, guiding the design of processable polymer networks.

Dynamic Injectable Hydrogels

We study how dynamic bonds govern flow, molecular damage and structural recovery in injectable hydrogels for advanced biomedical applications.
