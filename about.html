Principal Investigator

Aaaliyah Dookhith

Assistant Professor of Chemical Engineering

Our research focuses on understanding how molecular-scale processes govern the mechanical performance, durability, and failure of polymeric materials. We combine tools from polymer physics, rheology, fracture mechanics, mechanochemistry, and materials characterization to uncover the relationships between molecular structure and macroscopic behavior.

Current interests include polymer mechanochemistry, soft adhesion and fracture, dynamic polymer networks, sustainable polymer materials, and advanced methods for quantifying molecular damage in soft matter.

Prior to joining the faculty, Aaaliyah completed a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering at the University of Texas at Austin and conducted postdoctoral research at Columbia University.
